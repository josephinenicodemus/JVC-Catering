Our Story

A Family Built on Flavour & Love

JVC Catering was born in the heart of Dar es Salaam from the hands of Mama Jacqueline Vuai — a woman whose passion for cooking was matched only by her generosity. Growing up preparing traditional Tanzanian feasts for family gatherings, Jacqueline discovered early that food was her greatest gift to the world.

In 2016, what began as home catering for neighbours and community celebrations slowly blossomed into something extraordinary. Word spread quickly — not just about the food, but about the warmth, the care, and the uncompromising quality that Jacqueline brought to every single plate.

Today, JVC Catering is proudly carried forward by Jacqueline's two daughters — Victoria and Christine — who grew up watching their mother transform ingredients into moments of joy. Together, this trio of remarkable women have honoured every cherished family recipe while bringing modern presentation and professional service to Tanzania's most prestigious events.

From intimate family celebrations to grand wedding receptions and corporate galas, JVC remains a family business at heart — where every guest is treated like family and every dish is prepared with genuine love.
